Biography

Welcome to my website! My name is Nathan Hammer and I am from Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. I am currently pursuing a B.A. in Digital Media, Arts, and Technology @ Penn State Behrend. Most of my work involves computer programming, web development, graphic design, and server management. If you want to check out my resume; you can access it through the navigation bar below, or just by clicking here.

I have always been curious about the inner-workings of computers and video games. At a young age, I taught myself how to make levels for a game called Team Fortress 2, which has an art style designed around its mechanics. It sparked my passion for software that synergize creativity and technical design. Later in life, I experimented with creating game servers and addons for Garry's Mod, that has lead me to understand server infrastructure and programming. I am always exercising my skill set, in and outside of college. There is never a moment where I am not learning and applying new concepts. Nowadays, my goals tend to focus on creating new and interesting experiences through user interactivity, in order to teach and entertain others.
